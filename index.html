Welcome to Image Pixel Analyzer

Explore and analyze pixel values in images effortlessly.

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Click the "Choose File" button to select an image from your device.
  2. Hover over the image to see pixel values at your cursor position.
  3. Click anywhere on the image to copy the mouse position (X;Y) to your clipboard.
Move your mouse over the image to display mouse location and pixel values.
